About Us

Padmakanya Multiple Campus(PKMC) located in Bagbazar, Kathmandu, established in 1951. PKMC is Nepal's first and oldest women's college. A constitute campus of Tribhuvan University, offering Bachelor's and Master's degreein Huminities, Management and science, focused on women's empowerment with modern facilitiesand diverse programs like Women Studies and Hospitality Management.
